For today, don’t try to memorize the whole note. Just pick three sentences and repeat them.

For example:

“I can understand English, but I need more practice speaking.”

“I want to express my ideas more naturally.”

“I practice speaking a little bit every day.”

Say them out loud a few times. Then try to change one word and make your own sentences.

Small steps are better. Your brain is building a new path, one sentence at a time. 🌱
